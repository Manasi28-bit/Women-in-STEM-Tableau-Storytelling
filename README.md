## Overview
This Tableau storytelling project analyzes gender representation in STEM fields,
focusing on education trends, employment alignment, and salary disparities.

The dashboard answers key questions around:
- Why women are underrepresented in STEM
- How graduation trends have changed over time
- Whether women work in fields related to their education
- Salary differences by gender and ethnicity

---

## Key Insights
- Women have lower representation in Computer Science and Engineering roles.
- Graduation percentages for women in CS & Engineering declined between 2000â€“2015.
- Health-related fields show the highest alignment between education and employment.
- Salary gaps exist across gender and ethnicity in STEM professions.
